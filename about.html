Who are we?

Empowering Farmers The MORINGA project is a five-year initiative in South Africa aimed at enhancing the incomes of 4,000 farming households. Employing an inclusive market systems development approach, the project focuses on:​ BEAM Exchange Market Access: Connecting farmers to markets for moringa products. Capacity Building: Providing training on sustainable cultivation and processing techniques. Economic Inclusion: Ensuring marginalized groups benefit from the moringa value chain.
